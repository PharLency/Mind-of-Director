These constraints guide the model in choosing appropriate

------------------------------------------------------------
CONSTRAINT: CHARACTER STATE → CAMERA SUITABILITY
------------------------------------------------------------

state: {"static","walking"}

# 1️⃣ SINGLE-PERSON CAMERA CONSTRAINTS
------------------------------------------------------------
If state == "static":
    Preferred templates:
        ["singlestatic","push_in","push_out","orbit","crane",
         "pedestal","pan","tilt","roll","zoom","rack_focus"]
    Avoid:
        ["truck"]  # lateral tracking lacks motivation when subject is static.

    Preferred templates:
        ["truck","push_in","push_out","push_in_to_A","push_in_pair",
         "crane","pan","crosscut","full_shot"]
    Use with caution:
        ["orbit","pedestal","tilt","rack_focus"]
    Avoid:
        ["singlestatic","two_static","roll"]

# 2️⃣ TWO-PERSON CAMERA CONSTRAINTS
------------------------------------------------------------
If both characters are static:
    Preferred templates:
        ["two_static","push_in_pair","push_in_to_A",
         "orbit_duet","rack_focus_between","pedestal_duet","crosscut"]
    Avoid:
        ["truck_between"]  # best used when characters move or circle each other.

If one or both characters are walking:
    Preferred templates:
        ["truck_between","push_in_pair","push_in_to_A",
         "crane","crosscut","full_shot"]
    Use with caution:
        ["orbit_duet","pedestal_duet"]
    Avoid:
        ["two_static"]

# 3️⃣ GROUP CAMERA CONSTRAINTS
------------------------------------------------------------
If multiple characters (≥3):
    Always prefer:
        ["full_shot"]
    Reason:
        Full-shot best establishes spatial relations and ensemble movement.

GENERAL LOGIC
------------------------------------------------------------
- Static moments focus on composition, eye direction, and emotion.
- Walking or moving moments emphasize parallax, following motion,
  and spatial continuity.
- Avoid using stationary setups (e.g., singlestatic, two_static)
  for dynamic walking scenes unless dramatically motivated.
- "truck" and "push" styles integrate best with movement.
- "roll" and "orbit" are stylistic; reserve for special beats.
